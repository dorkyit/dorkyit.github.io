Dorky IT started in my brain in 2009 with one belief: be honest. Over 15 years later, I am still living it.

Our Story

From Server Room to Full-Service MSP

Dorky IT was founded by a systems engineer who spent decades managing enterprise infrastructure for Small-Medium Businesses, large enterprises and government entities. He kept running into the same frustration: vendors who over promsie the world and underdeliver after the PO is cut.

Dorky IT was launched with commitment to doing things differently. The core idea is simple: be honest and upfront in the what, when, where, why and how.

How We Think

Proactive IT Management

The break-fix model is a bad deal for everyone. You pay for problems that could have been prevented. We flipped the incentive: our job is to make sure nothing breaks.

01

Prevention Over Reaction

We monitor your systems around the clock and address anomalies before they become outages. Most clients never even know a problem existed, because we handled it before 9 AM.

02

Transparency Without Jargon

We report on what we do every month. You'll always know the state of your IT, in plain language. No smoke, no upselling, just an honest picture of your environment.

03

Strategic, Not Just Tactical

We don't just keep things running. Our consulting service helps clients plan IT investments, navigate software decisions, and build infrastructure that scales with the business.
